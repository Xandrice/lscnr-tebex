# Support

Need help with a purchase, package delivery, or a custom order?

## Discord

The fastest way to get help is through our Discord server. Create a payment support ticket in the **create a ticket** section with:

- Your CFX username
- Tebex transaction ID (if applicable)
- Package name and issue description

For **clothing, billboards, car liveries, and other in-game customizations**, open a payment support ticket on Discord — we cannot list prices for custom work before the customization is made.

## Purchase issues

If a package did not deliver:

1. Confirm you completed checkout and received a Tebex receipt email.
2. Verify you logged in with the correct FiveM account before purchase.
3. Restart FiveM and rejoin the server.

## Refunds

If you are not satisfied you may request a refund within 24 hours. Chargebacks or PayPal disputes result in a permanent ban from all of our services.
