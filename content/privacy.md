# Privacy Policy

## What we collect

- CFX/FiveM username when you authenticate via Tebex
- Discord identity if you choose Discord login (handled by Tebex)
- Order metadata stored when Tebex webhooks are configured (transaction ID, packages, total)

## What we do not collect

- Payment card numbers (handled entirely by Tebex)
- Passwords (authentication is via Tebex OAuth)

## How we use data

- Deliver purchased packages in-game
- Display order history on your account page
- Provide customer support

## Third parties

- **Tebex** — payment processing and basket authentication
- **Vercel** — website hosting
- **Discord** — community support (optional widget/member counts)

## Contact

For privacy questions, reach out via Discord support.
